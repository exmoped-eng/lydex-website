SpellStar

Gamified spelling practice for UK primary school children aged 4–11.

About the App

SpellStar makes spelling practice fun and effective. Aligned with the Department for Education statutory word lists, it covers the full UK primary curriculum from Reception to Year 6. Children practise their weekly spelling words through engaging game modes that build confidence and retention.

Import spelling lists by taking a photo, typing them in, sharing a code, or scanning a QR. Teachers can manage classroom lists, and parents can track progress — all without any data leaving the device.

Status: In development.

Pricing: Free tier with Spell Cast mode. Paid tiers from £3.49/month for full access to all game modes and features.

Features

13 Game Modes

From Spell Cast to word scrambles, each mode reinforces spelling in a different way.

Weekly Spelling Import

Photo, type, share code, or QR — four ways to get spelling lists into the app.

Curriculum Aligned

Built around DfE statutory word lists for Reception through Year 6.

Teacher & Classroom Mode

Manage lists for a whole class with simple share codes.

Parent Progress Reports

PIN-gated parent area with detailed progress tracking across all word lists.

Avatars & Badges

Customisable avatars and a badge system to keep children motivated.

Privacy

SpellStar stores all data locally on the device. No cloud, no external APIs, no tracking, no analytics. The app is designed to be compliant with COPPA and the UK Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code). Only a child's first name, school year, learning progress, and a hashed PIN are stored — and none of it ever leaves the device.
