Mills Through Time — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Overview

Mills Through Time is developed and published by Lydex Solutions Ltd. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects (in short: none) and how it operates.

Data Collection

Mills Through Time does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is requested or recorded
  • No account creation is required
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are collected
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or external services of any kind. No data is shared with any third party.

Internet Connectivity

Mills Through Time does not require an internet connection to function. The app operates entirely offline. No network requests are made at any time during use.

Advertising

The app contains no advertisements of any kind.

In-App Purchases

Mills Through Time offers an optional premium unlock through the App Store. This transaction is handled entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's own privacy policy. Lydex Solutions does not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Because Mills Through Time collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. No special provisions for children's data are necessary as no data is collected.
