Mills Through Time

Nine Men's Morris across the ages — a strategy board game spanning 7 historical eras.

About the Game

Mills Through Time brings the ancient strategy game of Nine Men's Morris to life through 7 beautifully crafted historical eras — from the Caveman age to UltraModern. Each era features unique themed boards, game pieces, and AI opponents with their own personality.

Play through Career mode to unlock new eras, or jump straight into Quick Play. Challenge yourself against the clock in Time Challenge mode, or pass the device and play against a friend in Local 2-Player.

Status: Coming soon to the App Store.

Pricing: Free with optional premium unlock (£3.99) for additional eras.

Features

4 Game Modes

Career, Quick Play, Time Challenge, and Local 2-Player.

7 Historical Eras

Caveman, Ancient Egypt, Roman, Medieval, Victorian, Modern, and UltraModern — each with unique artwork.

14 Themed Game Pieces

Two sets of pieces per era, designed to fit the time period.

Smart AI

4 difficulty levels with themed AI opponents that adapt to your skill.
