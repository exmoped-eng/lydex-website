Mills Through Time — Support

Frequently asked questions and how to get help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock additional eras?

The base game includes free eras to play. You can unlock all 7 eras with a one-time premium purchase (£3.99) from within the app. No subscription required.

Does the app need an internet connection?

No. Mills Through Time works entirely offline. You can play anywhere without Wi-Fi or mobile data.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Mills Through Time collects no personal data, has no analytics, no ads, and no third-party SDKs. See our privacy policy for full details.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" and sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Your premium unlock will be restored at no additional cost.

The app is not loading or is crashing.

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure your device is running the latest version of iOS
  • Check the App Store for any available updates to Mills Through Time
  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, uninstall and reinstall the app (your purchase can be restored)

What devices are supported?

Mills Through Time requires an iPhone or iPad running iOS 16 or later.

How do I play against a friend?

Select "Local 2-Player" from the game mode menu. Both players take turns on the same device.

Still need help?

If your question is not answered above, email us at ed@lydexsolutions.co.uk and we will get back to you within 1–2 working days.

When contacting us, it helps to include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • A description of the issue
